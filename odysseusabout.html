About

The Odyssey is one of the greatest pieces of literature to ever be written. Masterfully crafted by the blind poet Homer, 2000 years ago, The Odyssey has captured hearts for generations. Odysseus’s story is complex, and is full of twists and turns. I set out to create a sort of itinerary of Odysseus’s travels, which is displayed in real time on this website. I hope you enjoy my creation, and check back in 10 years for the epic finale.
